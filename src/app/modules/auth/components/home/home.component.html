About Us


When we design software, we design for every stakeholder.


We build software to fulfil two simple business goals -

     • A business who extends credit expects that the customer pays back on time.

     • A customer who takes credit pays by the due date when reminded respectfully.


Our software follows 3 simple design thinking principles

     1. Simple to use

     2. Connect all

     3. Important Information first


If your business is facing difficulties in managing their credit transactions with customers, including tracking credit utilization, generating statements and bills, timely collections and reconciling payments, then do reach out to us.


Our software platform offer following features:

     • User Management: Login, registration, profile management

     • Credit Management: Track and manage credit transactions between gas stations and fleet customers, credit limit setting, credit utilization tracking

     • Billing and Statement: Generate credit statements and bills, payment tracking and reconciliation

     • Reports and Analytics: Generate reports and analytics on credit utilization and payment history

     • Notifications and Reminders: Automated reminders for payment due, credit outstanding notifications

Adding Credit Purchases

Business owners will be able to add credit purchases made by customers by sending a message to the bot with the details of the purchase. The bot will prompt the user to enter the customer’s name, vehicle license plate number, amount of the purchase, and the date of the purchase.

Viewing Credit Purchases:

Business owners will be able to view a list of all credit purchases made by customers by sending a message to the bot with the command to view purchases. The bot will display a list of all credit purchases, along with the customer’s name, vehicle license plate number, amount, and date of each purchase.

Adding Credit Payments:

Business owners will be able to add credit payments made by customers by sending a message to the bot with the details of the payment. The bot will prompt the user to enter the customer’s name, amount of the payment, and the date of the payment.

Viewing Credit Payments:

Business owners will be able to view a list of all credit payments made by customers by sending a message to the bot with the command to view payments. The bot will display a list of all credit payments, along with the customer’s name, amount, and date of each payment.

Viewing Credit Ledgers:

Business owners will be able to view their credit ledger by sending a message to the bot with the command to view the ledger. The bot will display the total credit purchases, payments, and outstanding credit balance for each customer and vehicle.

Tracking Credit Outstanding:

Business owners will be able to track the credit outstanding against both customers and vehicles by sending a message to the bot with the command to track outstanding credit. The bot will display the credit outstanding balance for each customer.
