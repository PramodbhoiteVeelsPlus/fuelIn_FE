About Us


When we design software, we design for every stakeholder.


We build software to fulfil two simple business goals -

     • A business who extends credit expects that the customer pays back on time.

     • A customer who takes credit pays by the due date when reminded respectfully.


Our software follows 3 simple design thinking principles

     1. Simple to use

     2. Connect all

     3. Important Information first


If your business is facing difficulties in managing their credit transactions with customers, including tracking credit utilization, generating statements and bills, timely collections and reconciling payments, then do reach out to us.


Our software platform offer following features:

     • User Management: Login, registration, profile management

     • Credit Management: Track and manage credit transactions between gas stations and fleet customers, credit limit setting, credit utilization tracking

     • Billing and Statement: Generate credit statements and bills, payment tracking and reconciliation

     • Reports and Analytics: Generate reports and analytics on credit utilization and payment history

     • Notifications and Reminders: Automated reminders for payment due, credit outstanding notifications
